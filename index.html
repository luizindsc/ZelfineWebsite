Who is Zelfine?

Zelfine, the Brazilian 3D artist behind VRChat’s most-played map,  Popcorn Palace, is a passionate creator known for crafting immersive worlds and custom avatars. Their talent for 3D design has made them a standout in the VRChat community, bringing people together through engaging virtual experiences.

Delphy Kobo

The Delphy Kobo Avatar is an original creation by Zelfine, showcasing their unique and creative design style. This avatar is a vibrant and expressive character with a playful and energetic personality.

Brazilian Miku Outfit

The Brazilian Miku Outfit by Zelfine is a creative and stylish take on the iconic Hatsune Miku character, reimagined with a Brazilian carnival-inspired theme.

Hanami Onsen

The Hanami Onsen Map by Zelfine is a breathtaking virtual environment inspired by the serene beauty of Japanese hot springs (onsen) during the cherry blossom (hanami) season.
